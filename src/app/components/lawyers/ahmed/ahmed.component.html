He is an Emirati lawyer, writer and editor-in-chief. He is a founding member of the Emirates Human Rights Association and a member of the UAE Bloggers Union. He has many literary and legal publications that are an important reference for lawyers. He has extensive legal experience of more than 20 years. His previous work in the Public Prosecution made him familiar with administrative and governmental work procedures. He is one of the lawyers known for his proficiency in commercial, labor, personal status, civil and criminal cases.
